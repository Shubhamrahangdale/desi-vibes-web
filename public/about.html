About EventMitra

Empowering event organizers across India since 2020

Our Story

EventMitra was born from a simple idea: making event management accessible and effortless for everyone in India. Founded in 2020, we've grown from a small startup to India's premier event management platform, serving thousands of event organizers across 25+ cities.

Our Mission

We believe that every event, big or small, deserves to be memorable. Our mission is to provide the tools, support, and technology that empower event organizers to create unforgettable experiences for their attendees.
